OUR MISSION

The goal of this project is to offer the Queen's Hyperloop Design Team with machine-learning integrated camera system, capable of detecting and localizing potential obstructions in the pod's path

THE TEAM

We are a group of aspiring engineers interested in machine learning and the future transportation. From a diverse mix of engineering specialities, ranging from Mechanical Engineering to Applied Mathematics, our unique combination of skills alongside the team's fostered culture of creativity and innovation has allowed us to successfully complete the project.
